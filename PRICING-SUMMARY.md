## 💰 Pricing Philosophy

**Why these prices?**

1. **Starter (€1,900)**: Covers consultation and rapid prototyping with basic documentation
2. **Explorer (€4,900)**: Full discovery process with architecture design and proof-of-concept
3. **Professional (€14,900)**: Production system with integrations, testing, and post-launch support
4. **Enterprise (€39,900+)**: Complex multi-system deployment with compliance and long-term support
5. **Research (Custom)**: Aligned with academic budgets and grant cycles

**What's included in every tier:**
- Direct access to our engineering team
- No recurring license fees
- You own the code and models
- Transparent pricing with no hidden costs

**What's NOT included:**
- Third-party API costs (OpenAI, etc.)
- Cloud hosting fees (if using cloud)
- Hardware costs (if on-prem)
- Post-support period maintenance (available as add-on)
